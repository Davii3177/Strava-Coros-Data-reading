About Gaman AI

Built around the training data runners already create.

Gaman AI brings Strava and Coros activities into one practical view for pace trends, workout planning, race goals, and recovery check-ins.

Recommendations show the evidence available and call out missing data. Recovery guidance is educational and does not replace a coach or clinician.
