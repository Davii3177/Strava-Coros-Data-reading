ABOUT GAMAN AI

Built for runners who want the signal, not the noise.

Gaman AI brings Strava and Coros activity data into one focused console. It turns your training history into understandable pace trends, useful workouts, race planning, and practical recovery check-ins.

Use it as a thoughtful training companion — not a replacement for a coach or clinician.
